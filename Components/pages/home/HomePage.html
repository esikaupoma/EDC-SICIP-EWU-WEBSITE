About EDC SICIP

The Executive Development Center (EDC), under the Skills for Industry Competitiveness and Innovation Program (SICIP), has partnered with East West University (EWU) to offer specialized training in key industries. On October 2, 2024, EDC launched its Graduate Diploma in Leather, Leather Goods, and Footwear Management program. The program aims to equip professionals with industry-relevant skills through a blend of theoretical and practical learning, preparing them for leadership roles in the leather and footwear sectors.
